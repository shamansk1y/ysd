Who we are

York Snack Food Distr. Inc., based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, has been a key player in the U.S. snack market for over 20 years. Specializing in chips, snacks, and candy, the company distributes popular brands such as Wise, Frito Lay, Utz, Mr. G, Rap Snack, and Andy Capp's. Known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, York Snack Food has built a loyal customer base by offering a diverse selection of tasty treats that cater to all tastes and preferences.
